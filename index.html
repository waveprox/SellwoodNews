New Grading System, Students and Faculty Enraged and Confused

This year also brings a new grading system. Instead of just getting A's, B's, or C's, students will now get grades based on how well they meet the learning goals in each class. The new system focuses more on growth and understanding, not just test scores. Teachers will give more feedback to help you know exactly what you need to work on, and there will be more chances to improve your grades.

Mental Health Support Gets Major Upgrade This Semester

For mental health, there are more ways to get support if you're feeling stressed, anxious, or down. You can visit a counselor, use a wellness room to relax, or take classes that teach you how to handle your emotions and build better friendships.
